Vedanth 10.0

About the exhibition

Vedanth 10.0 is the online technical exhibition of TechTatvaâ€™20 which will be conducted over a span of 3 days. Teams will present their projects in the form of descriptive videos in the fields of IOT, machine learning, medical innovation, robotics and automation, technology for social betterment, smart learning and frugal innovation. Our latest addition is a COVID-19 corner where special emphasis will be given to COVID-19 based projects. Winners shall be declared based on the creativity and novelty of the innovation. Winners for Vedanth will be selected from participating teams of day 1 and day 2.


Participation Criteria:


  • Video-based presentation with a step-wise description of the working project.
  • Projects can be: Software / Hardware / Simulation-based.

    • Video Description:


  • Real-life application of the project
  • If the project is:
    (a) A bot: Record the complete working of the bot
    (b) Simulation/Software-based: Running of the simulation/code of the software must be recorded (Complete code is not required)
  • Time Limit: 5-15 minutes
  • The video, once submitted is FINAL. No changes will be entertained
  • Plagiarised and unoriginal content will be rejected
